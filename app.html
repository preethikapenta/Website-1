Who is an app developer??

Application developers are those who write software programs for use across mobile operating systems,including Apple,Android and Windows devices.

What are his/her roles?

They are responsible for designing and coding functional software programs and applications.They collaborate with teams to set specifications ,write high-quality code,conduct testing and troubleshoot applications..
