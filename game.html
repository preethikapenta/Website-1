Who is a game developer??

Game developers are those who write code for games for a variety of formats such as PCs ,consoles ,web browsers and mobile phones.

What are his roles?

Actually,They help transform games from a concept with rules,goals with perfect design to a playable reality by coding visual elemetns,programming features and testing iterations until a game is ready for market.

How can you get through?

  • The one who was interested in playing video games and has much knowledge about computers can get through this.
  • The games coding language is chosen following the development of the game's initial design and these are developed by variety of programming languages depending upon the platform (console,computer,mobile) and scope of the game.
  • Major languages used are c++,java,c#.

Things you have to learn and explore..

  • Recquires keen observation upon various games and should recquire good creativity for design of the game.
  • Explore by small small games from the start even by using html and javascript.

The range of salaries..

The salary of a game developer ranges between 1 lakh to 14.1 lakhs with an average annual salary of 5.9 lakhs.
