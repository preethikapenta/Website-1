Who is a data scientist or analyst??

A Data scientist uses data to understand and explain the phenomena around them , and help organizations make better decisions.

What are his roles?

  • They usually find patterns and trends in datasets to uncover insights.
  • They create algorithms and datamodels to forecast outcomes.
  • Stay on top recommendations to other teams and senior staff.
